Information of Midterm Exam #3


Exam Instructions [Link]

  • Coverage: All topics covered from week #8 to week #11 (from the concepts of testing hypothesis to one-sample, two-sample, and paired t tests).

  • Exam Schedule:
    Thursday, 11/13/2025
      Note:For those who requested accommodation, please sign up for a time slot with the OEA Proctoring Center at https://westchester-accommodate.symplicity.com/ as early as possible.

  • Format: There will be multiple-choice questions and word problems. Everyone will have a slightly different problem set with similar types of problems.

  • Length: The exam takes 75 minutes.

  • Logistics:
    • 1. Bring your laptop to class to take the exam on D2L.
    • 2. You are also encouraged to print out flowcharts that are available on the course web page.
    • 3. You may prepare a one-page formula sheet on a standard sheet of printer paper.

  • Make-up exam. There will be no makeup midterm exam. See the syllabus for the policies of the makeup exam.
